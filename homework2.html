Education

I attended Saint. Augustines school for K-5th and then moved to Saint. Michaels school for 6th-8th grade. From there, I attended Central Catholic High School which was the private catholic school near me. In high school I was involved in many different things. I was the leader of a club called Visions Coffehouse. We would create a magazine of students art work, writings, pictures, and more and then promote then at a coffeehosue where students can come and share some sort of talent with the school. This is how I decided I wanted to be a journalism major because I loved editing the magazine and helping other students with their work that they wanted to publish in the magazine.
